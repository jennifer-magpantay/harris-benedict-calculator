Why do you need that?

This application does not replace an appointment with your GP or your Nutritionist. It is just a representation of a calculation that gives an estimative based on a study. But, if you are feeling any side effects from your day-to-day routine, for example, excessive tiredness, lack of sleep or appetite, or want to achieve a goal, such as gain or loose weight, you must contact them to understand what is going on with your health and to get the correct advice.
