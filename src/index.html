What is the Daily Calorie calculator?

It is a mathematical equation for estimating the total calories a person needs, known as Total Energy Expenditure (TEE), considering their Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Physical Activities Level (PAL).

What is Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)?

It is the energy a person needs to carry out to complete fundamental metabolic functions, such as brain function or breath, in a period of time, without considering the energy that is spent on activities of the day. It is calculated by using the Harris-Benedict equation.

What is the Harris-Benedic Equation?

It is an equation that takes into consideration height, weight, age and gender to estimate an individual's basal metabolic rate.

The result of this equation multiplied by the PAL factor is used to calculate TEE.

What is Physical Activities Level (PAL)?

PAL is a number multiplier used to estimate a person's TEE in combination with the BMR. It can be also used to compute the amount of food energy a person needs to consume in order to maintain a particular lifestyle.
